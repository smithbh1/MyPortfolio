Commerce Mission

Our mission is to create a solid foundation for our community. We know the key to this is investing resources in local businesses and provide a network of success. If one of us wins, we all win. Let's build our community together!

Upcoming Events

07/31 - 08/07

This is what we all have been waiting for the past year - the famous Santaquin Orchard Days! This is a time to celebrate Santaquin and its farmers who have helped us so much! Come join us from 07/31 - 08/07 to celebrate our heritage!

08/02 - Santaquin Rodeo

One of our long-standing traditions is the Orchard Days Rodeo! With multiple events like calf roping, mutton bustin', and a dirt bike show that last the night, there is never a dull moment!

08/03 - Santaquin Pageant

The Santaquin Pageant is perfect for young women ages 6-16 to represent the best attributes Santaquin has to offer.

Local Activities

Tractor Tour

Because of the amount of farms and orchards thoughout Santaquin, we offer many tours and "U-Pick" opportunities to get you the fresh produce you need.

Hiking

Another great activity is hiking the great Santaquin Canyon! With only being 3.2 miles roundtrip with a 1700ft. elevation gain, it can be a great outdoor adventure for the whole family!
